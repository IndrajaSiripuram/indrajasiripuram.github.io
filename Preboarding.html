Walmart Global Tech Preboarding

The Preboarding application is designed to create a seamless and engaging experience for new hires before their first day. It provides them with important resources, introductions, and tasks to complete, helping them feel welcomed and prepared. Through personalized content, checklists, and communication tools, the application ensures that new team members are informed, connected, and ready to start their journey with confidence and ease.

Project type:End-to-end Pre-boarding mobile and web application

Role:UX/UI designer

Tools:Figma, Figjam, Mural Board

Duration:Q4 2023

1.Problem

The existing preboarding process faced several challenges, including:

  • Emails getting lost in hiring managers' inboxes.
  • Delays caused by incomplete information (e.g., missing SSNs).
  • Manual processes for equipment requests, mapping user IDs, and assigning software.
  • Inconsistent onboarding for remote associates, causing delays in equipment delivery.
  • Lack of a dedicated support channel for new hires during preboarding, leaving them feeling isolated or uncertain about next steps.

    • These inefficiencies resulted in a fragmented experience for hiring managers and new hires, impacting productivity and satisfaction.

    2.Research & Journey Mapping

    For the Preboarding app, we conducted research with multiple new hires and associates, deeply empathizing with their pain points. We prioritized a user-centered approach, keeping their needs at the forefront throughout the design process. By gathering continuous feedback, we ensured that the experience is intuitive, engaging, and tailored to effectively address user challenges.

    Key Outcomes

    The redesigned preboarding application delivered measurable improvements:

    Efficiency Gains: Automation reduced manual tasks, allowing hiring managers to focus on strategic activities.

    Improved Onboarding: Remote associates received equipment on time, enhancing their first-day experience.

    Enhanced Visibility: Dashboards provided stakeholders with real-time insights, reducing bottlenecks.

    New Hire Support: The onboarding buddy system reduced uncertainty for new hires, increasing their confidence and preparedness.

    Positive Feedback: Hiring managers praised the intuitive interface and time-saving features, while new hires appreciated the personalized support.

    Challenges and Learnings

    One challenge was balancing automation with flexibility for edge cases (e.g., high-end equipment requests). Additionally, ensuring the onboarding buddy system had clear guidelines and workflows required cross-functional collaboration.
