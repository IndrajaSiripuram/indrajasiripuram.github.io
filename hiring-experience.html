Provides a platform to adopt and list a pet easily.

OVERVIEW

Adopt is a user-friendly website that simplifies the pet adoption process by allowing users to complete all necessary formalities online. It caters to individuals who may feel unable to adequately care for their pets by providing a platform to create detailed posts about their pets and share them on the website. Interested parties can then schedule a convenient pick-up, either from the owner's home or a nearby rescue center.
The design of the website was meticulously crafted after extensive research and testing, with a strong emphasis on addressing the specific needs and preferences of users. The goal was to create a seamless and efficient experience for both those looking to rehome their pets and those seeking to adopt.

THE PROBLEM

Finding a preferred pet is challenging; adoption procedures are time-consuming and frustrating due to cluttered websites and complex functionality. Our platform aims to simplify this process, offering a streamlined and user-friendly experience to alleviate these issues.

THE SOLUTION

The Adopt website simplifies the adoption process by offering users a wide range of pet options and filtering capabilities based on their specific requirements. This user-friendly platform aims to enhance the overall adoption experience, making it easier for individuals to find their ideal pet.

DESIGN PROCESS

USER RESEARCH SUMMARY

Conducted user interviews to know about the users pain points and what they feel would have made their experience easy.

Study type: Moderated usability study

Number of participants: 5

Location: Coffee shop

Duration: 20 min

PAIN POINTS

1.Time Taking

Manually searching for pets can be time-consuming and requires significant patience, with no guarantee of how long it will take to find the right one.

2.Confused

Dog owners and rescuers often face challenges in finding new homes for their pets without knowing where to start.

3.Frustration

The abundance of content and frequent pop-up ads on websites can be highly frustrating for users.

4.Pick up

Despite completing all the necessary processes, there may be limitations in scheduling a pickup at our convenience.

PAPER WIREFRAMES

The primary objective of the website was to simplify the pet adoption process for users and facilitate easy donations. Additionally, we aimed to create a website that is straightforward and user-friendly, ensuring easy navigation for visitors.

The wireframes were created by sketching various alternative options for each screen, quickly identifying the best choices. These selected options were then consolidated into a single screen, forming a comprehensive wireframe.

LOW-FIDELITY PROTOTYPE

To ensure a clear and consistent user experience, low-fidelity (lo-fi) prototypes were developed for different screens. These prototypes allow users to navigate through the website without any confusion. Additionally, the designs were slightly modified to adapt to different screen sizes while maintaining continuity throughout the website.

USABILITY STUDY FINDINGS

To enhance the design process, we conducted two rounds of unmoderated usability testing with a total of 5 participants. The findings from the initial study played a crucial role in guiding the transition from wireframes to mockups. Subsequently, the second study utilized high-fidelity prototypes to identify areas in the mockups that required further refinement. This iterative approach ensured the incorporation of user feedback and improvement of the overall user experience.

Interaction design

PET ADOPTION PROCESS

To enhance user convenience, the website offers multiple clickable options throughout the page, allowing users to easily navigate to the adoption page from any location on the site. This feature ensures that users have quick and convenient access to the adoption process regardless of their current location on the page.

The adoption process involves two main steps: searching for a pet and filling out a detailed form. Users can search for their desired pet by utilizing various search criteria. Once a suitable pet is found, users can proceed to fill out a form that includes all the necessary details. This form also provides flexibility in selecting a preferred time for pet pickup, accommodating the user's schedule.

RESPONSIVE WEB DESIGN

Consistency was a key consideration throughout the design process, ensuring a cohesive user experience. The screens were designed to be responsive and adaptable to multiple devices, following a mobile-first approach. This approach prioritizes the design and optimization for mobile devices, while also ensuring a seamless transition and consistent user interface across various screen sizes and resolutions.

FILTERS TO SAVE USERS TIME

Despite its seemingly small impact, fixing the filter tab had a significant positive effect on the user experience. This simple change enhanced usability by providing users with easy and constant access to the filtering functionality, allowing them to refine their search criteria and find the desired results more efficiently.

The fixed filter button greatly improved user comfort and convenience. By remaining visible at all times on the screen, users could easily access and modify the filters without having to scroll back to the top of the page. This enhancement enabled a seamless filtering experience, empowering users to refine their search criteria from anywhere on the page.
