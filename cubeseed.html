OVERVIEW

Cubeseed is an innovative virtual marketplace driven by contracts, specifically designed to enhance agro-trade and promote agricultural growth in Africa. Our primary focus is on empowering farmers and establishing investment security. Leveraging our web and mobile applications, we seamlessly connect various stakeholders within the agricultural ecosystem, ensuring enhanced security, transparency, and connectivity through the power of technology. Our ultimate objective is to foster connections within the agricultural value chain, encouraging specialization, and attracting sustained investments, all while providing a reliable framework for investment security.

THE PROBLEM

Over 180 million smallholder farmers make up 90% of the farming population and are responsible for 80% of the food produced. However, they are plagued by a lack of access to credit, quality inputs, and markets. These challenges have a direct impact on farmers’ income, food security, food quality, and food affordability.

THE SOLUTION

Cubeseed offers a comprehensive platform that enables farmers to connect with investors, allowing them to maximize their profits with complete transparency. Additionally, Cubessed assists investors by providing them with detailed information and valuable insights, presenting them with the best investment opportunities.

USER RESEARCH & PERSONA

After conducting thorough research involving approximately 10 existing investors as well as new investors, we have gained a clear understanding of the challenges they are facing. Based on the results we obtained, we have developed a persona to represent their needs and preferences. Our research methodology included surveys and in-depth studies to gather comprehensive insights.

AFFINITY DIAGRAM

Given the geographical dispersion of our team across different countries, we opted to utilize the Affinity mapping technique. This approach enabled us to effectively organize data and ideas by their relationships and similarities. By leveraging this method, we fostered collaboration, identified patterns, and prioritized information, enabling us to work cohesively despite our physical distance.

DESIGN SYSTEM

DIGITAL WIREFRAMES

Our primary objective is to develop a website that enables investors to discover promising farmers for investment opportunities, ultimately leading to profitable returns.

The wireframes were designed by sketching some quick alternatives to each screen and the best options were marked and are put together in a single screen and formed a wireframe.

USABILITY STUDY FINDINGS

We conducted two usability studies, one unmoderated and one moderated, with a total of 10 participants. The website was designed to be used by both literate and illiterate investors, so we had five participants in each category. We made changes to the app based on the feedback we received, including repositioning some elements, changing colors, and adding new functionalities.

Here is a more detailed breakdown of the changes we made:

Repositioning of elements: We repositioned some of the elements on the website's home screen to make them easier to find. For example, we moved the "Invest Now" button to a more prominent location.

Changing colors: We changed the colors of some of the wesite's elements to make them more accessible to people with vision impairments. For example, we assigned each section on the home screen different color to make it easy to read.

Adding new functionalities: We added some new functionalities to the website, such as the ability to save featured farmers and track investment progress.

We believe that the changes we made will make the app more user-friendly and accessible to a wider range of investors. We are grateful for the feedback we received from the participants in our usability studies, and we will continue to make improvements to the app based on their feedback.

INTERACTION DESIGN

SIGN UP PROCESS

To ensure consistency, all six user groups in Cubeseed follow a similar sign-up process. This approach maintains a seamless experience across the platform, catering to the unique needs of each group while providing a unified user experience.

HIGH-FIDELITY PROTOTYPE

TAKE AWAYS

Although the website and app are currently in the development phase, we conducted recent research with investors using high-fidelity prototypes. The feedback we received was overwhelmingly positive, and investors expressed eagerness to try the app and website once they are launched. We have already received an enormous response for early registration and updates after the launch announcement, further fueling our excitement to introduce new features and enhancements
